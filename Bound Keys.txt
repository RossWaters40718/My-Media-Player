My Media Player 4.8 Bound Keyboard Keys

Play - Keyboard Play(XF86AudioPlay)
Next - Keyboard Next(XF86AudioNext)
Previous - Keyboard Previous(XF86AudioPrev)
Volume Up - Keyboard Volume Up(XF86AudioRaiseVolume)
Volume Down - Keyboard Volume Down(XF86AudioLowerVolume)
Toggle Mute - m, M, Keyboard Mute(XF86AudioMute)
Toggle Pause - p, P
Toggle Repeat - r, R
Stop - q, Q
Exit App. - e, E

(Audio/Video Modes Only)
Toggle Show Mode - w, W
Toggle Full Screen - f, F
Backward 10 Seconds - Left Arrow
Forward 10 Seconds - Right Arrow
Backward 60 Seconds - Down Arrow
Forward 60 Seconds - Up Arrow
